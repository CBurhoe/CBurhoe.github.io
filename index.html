CardioCrisis ECG Project

This spring, I worked with three other undergraduate computer science students on CardioCrisis ECG, a Raspberry Pi powered ECG monitor which works with Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to stream real-time ECG recording data to a practitioner's phone.
