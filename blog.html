CardioCrisis ECG Project - May 2025

This spring, I worked with three other undergraduate computer science students on CardioCrisis ECG, a Raspberry Pi powered ECG monitor which works with Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to stream real-time ECG recording data to a practitioner's phone. We presented our project for The Hub, which you can find here.
