About Me

I graduated from Johns Hopkins University in the Spring of 2025 with a B.S. in Computer Science and Cognitive Science. My interests include cybersecurity, cloud development, and web development.


During my time at Hopkins, I worked on various engineering projects, such as developing a mobile real-time ECG monitoring system. Since graduating, I’ve also competed in cybersecurity challenges hosted by Correlation One and TryHackMe.


This website serves as both my portfolio and a place to share technical writing.
